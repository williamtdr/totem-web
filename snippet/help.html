Help

Sorry to see you're having trouble! For a quick run-down of the site, see the basic tutorial. If the problem you're having isn't listed here, feel free to contact us.

Adding a Song to the Queue

  1. Make sure you're signed in first: your username should show in the upper right hand corner. If you haven't logged in yet, click the log in link and connect to your YouTube account.
  2. Click the Library tab at the top. Select an existing playlist to add music from, or click "Search Youtube" to find a song through its name.
  3. Next to the song's entry in the list, click "Add to Queue". The song will play in the room when it's your turn. You can see your position by clicking on the "x in queue" indicator above the chat.

Creating a Room

  1. From the tabs at the top, click "Rooms".
  2. Click "Create Room".
  3. Set a title and description for the room. If you'd like to make the room private, flip the switch and set a password.
  4. Click Create. Others can now join and queue songs in your room. To change additional room settings, click the cog in the upper right next to the room name.

Changing Notification Settings

You must be logged in to change desktop notification settings. Once you are, click your name in the upper right to open the settings menu. The selectors change which events you are notified on.

Editing & Viewing Profiles

You can change your profile settings from the menu in the upper right. Click on the "Choose Files" button to upload a custom avatar, and change the information fields to tell people about yourself. You can see other peoples' profiles by clicking on their names in the chat.

Skipping a Song

Type /skip in chat to skip the currently playing song and advance to the next one in the queue. For a full list of commands you can use, see /help.
